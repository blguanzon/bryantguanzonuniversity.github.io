We care about your privacy

Bryant Guanzon University (BGU) is committed to protecting your personal information. We collect both personal and non-personal data to provide services, communicate updates, improve our offerings, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell or rent your information and only share it with service providers under confidentiality agreements or as required by law. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, though absolute security cannot be guaranteed. You have rights to access, correct, opt-out, and request deletion of your information.
