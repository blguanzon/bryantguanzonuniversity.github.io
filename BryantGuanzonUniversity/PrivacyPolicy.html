Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16/07/2024

Bryant Guanzon University ("BGU," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information. This Data Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive through our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:

Information that identifies you personally, such as your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other identifiers that you provide voluntarily when you:

  • Register for events or courses
  • Apply for admission or employment
  • Subscribe to newsletters or other communications
  • Contact us with inquiries or requests

1.2 Non-Personal Information:

Information that does not directly identify you. This may include demographic data, aggregated information, and other anonymous data such as:

  • Browser and device information
  • IP addresses
  • Pages visited on the Site and the duration of visits

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

2.1 Providing Services:

To deliver the services you request, including course registration, admissions, and event participation.

2.2 Communication:

To communicate with you about university news, events, and updates, and to respond to your inquiries.

2.3 Improvement:

To improve our Site, services, and overall user experience through data analysis and research.

2.4 Compliance:

To comply with legal obligations and protect the rights and safety of BGU, our students, staff, and others.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

3.1 Service Providers:

Third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as web hosting, data analysis, and communication services, subject to confidentiality agreements.

3.2 Legal Requirements:

Government authorities or other entities when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BGU and others.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

5.1 Access and Correction:

Request access to and correct any personal information we hold about you.

5.2 Opt-Out:

Opt-out of receiving communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

5.3 Data Deletion:

Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our Site and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the Site after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
